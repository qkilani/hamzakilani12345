Alternative Exercises to Cable Tricep Pushdowns

If you're looking for an alternative to Cable Tricep Pushdowns, here are some effective exercises to target the triceps:

1. Tricep Dips (Bench or Machine Dips)

If you have access to a dip machine, it's an excellent alternative. Otherwise, you can perform bench dips by placing your hands on a bench and your feet on the floor (or elevated for added difficulty).

  • Sets/Reps: 3 sets x 8-12 reps

2. Overhead Tricep Extension (Dumbbell or Cable)

Using either a dumbbell or a cable machine, this exercise targets the long head of the triceps and allows for a deep stretch at the bottom.

  • Sets/Reps: 3 sets x 10-12 reps

3. Close-Grip Push-Ups

A great bodyweight alternative that really targets the triceps. Keep your hands close together (under your shoulders) to focus more on the triceps.

  • Sets/Reps: 3 sets x 10-15 reps

4. Skull Crushers (Lying Tricep Extensions)

This can be done with an EZ bar, dumbbells, or a straight barbell. It’s excellent for hitting all three heads of the triceps.

  • Sets/Reps: 3 sets x 8-10 reps

5. Tricep Kickbacks (Dumbbell or Cable)

This movement can be done with dumbbells or a cable machine, and it’s a great isolation exercise for the triceps, particularly the long head.

  • Sets/Reps: 3 sets x 12-15 reps

6. Diamond Push-Ups

This push-up variation places the hands in a diamond shape beneath the chest, which increases the focus on the triceps.

  • Sets/Reps: 3 sets x 10-20 reps
